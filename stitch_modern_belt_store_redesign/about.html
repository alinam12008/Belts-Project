Our History

1935

Foundation

Mr. Saeed Omar Basaffar (1935 – 2001) the founder was a self-made gentleman and known as a pioneer who had a leading strong personality. He realized the needs for the forthcoming of evolving industrial area which encouraged him to establish a private firm to sell vehicles spare parts including belts.

1959

Corporate Shift

Upon the quantum shift of business in 1959 he changed the name of the firm to Belt Store Trading in alignment with the new direction and the new vision of the Industrial needs for the Eastern Province.

1988

Leadership Expansion

Mr. Salem Basaffar (General Manager) is the eldest son of the late founder of the firm who had joined work beside with his father in 1988, initiating a period of rapid modernization.

2003

Digital Transformation

Mr. Mohammed Basaffar (Executive Manager) joined after acquiring his BS degree in Management Information System from University of South Florida, USA, bringing global technical standards to the operation.

2009

Modern Identity

Belts Store has adapted the new logo and corporate identity of the firm, solidifying its position as a primary entity to support industrial services.
